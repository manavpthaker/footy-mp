**RatingRing** — conic-gradient dial for a team's composite strength rating (0–99). Pair with FactorBars to break the rating down.

```jsx
<RatingRing value={69} />
<RatingRing value={88} size={56} label="Brazil" />
```

Props: `value`, `max` (default 99), `size` (px), `color`, `label`. The figure renders in the mono voice inside the hub.
